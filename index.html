Trips With Friends

by Keith G. Davis


Executive Summary


To understand the pain points of how people plan to travel in the future, post-Pandemic, and solve for one or more of those issues.

Recreational travelers enjoy planning and taking trips, but are hesitant on how much the experience will cost them and if they can afford it. How might we help recreational travelers, who are unsure of their financial means, to consider aspects like food, entertainment, admissions, and/or fuel when organizing a trip through proper travel planning and budgeting.

An app that allows users to plan trips with their friends, while budgeting the aspects of their itinerary.

UX Research & Designer
(Individual Contributor)


User Research


Objective 1

To understand the end-to-end process of how people make travel plans and what influences these decisions

Objective 2

Identify the most common issues people experience when planning a trip

Research Question:
What are the pain points of how people plan to travel in the future, post-Pandemic?

Objective 3

Understand the resources and tools people use for planning and why they like to use them
